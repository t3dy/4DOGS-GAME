# Canon — The Four Dogs

Canonical roster for DOGSGAME. These are Ted's real dogs; treat identity, breed, and named traits as immutable. Stats for gameplay (anxiety bar values, bladder capacity) are derived from these descriptions and live in `data/dogsim.db` once built.

## Okie

- **Breed:** Australian shepherd
- **Size:** standard (smaller than Tex)
- **Age class:** young/adult
- **Signature behaviors:**
  - Territorial pee marking (killed the real blueberry bush) — see `behaviors/B001_*.md`
  - Bone banker: stashes Old Roy rawhide bones under kiddie-pool-bed blankets in the garage — see `B005`
  - Anxious garage-seeker: garage is his refuge, not a punishment — see `B006`
  - Nighttime indoor-accidents if left in the house — see `B008`
  - Bark-chain instigator when paired with Tex in garage — see `B009`
  - Afternoon bed-joiner (when Ted is lying down, not sleeping) — see `B012`
- **Traits:**
  - High anxiety (stat axis; see B006)
  - Coprophagic (eats poop) → face-licks avoided
  - Face-licker-attempter (always tries when allowed)
- **Not:** Tex's belly-roll isn't Okie's thing. Distinct.

## Tex

## Attila

- **Breed:** Black Bernese / golden mountain cross
- **Size:** up to 100 lb — largest of the four
- **Coloring:** black with white paws and white chest puff
- **Age class:** adult
- **Signature behaviors:**
  - Squeaky-toy attention-signaling via pink pig / blue cow — see `B003`
  - Pre-walk whine (thunderous bark possible, but whines when leashed) — see `B010`
- **Traits:**
  - **Most mild-mannered, well-tempered, and best-behaved of the four.** Attentive and obedient — the dispositional counterweight to the Aussies and Crockett.
  - Bed-permitted (only dog allowed on the bed by default — see B013).
  - NOT flagged as coprophagic (per Ted's implication that face-licks from Attila are OK).
  - Big body, small voice in certain contexts (juxtaposition comedy — see B010).
  - Deliberate communicator (squeaky-toy signaling, paw-request — shows interiority and strategy).

## Household rules (affecting dogs)

- Aussies (Okie, Tex) are discouraged from the bed. Exception: rare ~monthly group TV/movie session with all four dogs — see `B011`.
- Face-licks from Okie, Tex, Crockett are avoided. Reason: coprophagia.
- Ted and partner both work and travel; dogs are sometimes confined to the garage for extended periods. Sitters, boarders, and travel-with-dogs are alternatives.
- Okie sleeps in the garage by default — his preference and Ted's practical solution to B008.

## Inter-dog relationships (known)

- Okie ↔ Tex: bark-chain amplification partners (B009). Both Aussies. Tex is the bigger brother.
- Attila ↔ Crockett: toy-broadcast target + thief (B003 → B004).
- Attila is the largest; Crockett is the oldest.
